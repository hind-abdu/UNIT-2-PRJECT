Jeddah

The Bride of the Red Sea - Where Tradition Meets Modernity

Explore Jeddah

Jeddah is a vibrant port city on the Red Sea, known for its historic Al-Balad district, stunning corniche, and modern shopping malls. As the gateway to the holy cities of Mecca and Medina, Jeddah blends tradition with contemporary life.

Al-Balad Historical District

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage site with its traditional coral houses.

King Fahd's Fountain

Witness the world's tallest fountain, reaching up to 312 meters.

Red Sea Beaches

Enjoy pristine beaches and coral reefs perfect for diving and snorkeling.

Jeddah Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

October to April offers the most pleasant weather for exploring Jeddah's outdoor attractions.

  • Winter (Nov-Feb): Mild temperatures, perfect for sightseeing
  • Spring (Mar-Apr): Warm but comfortable for beach activities
  • Summer (May-Sep): Very hot and humid, best for indoor activities

Getting Around

Jeddah has various transportation options to explore the city comfortably.

  • Taxis and ride-sharing services widely available
  • Rental cars for more flexibility
  • Walking ideal in Al-Balad historic area
  • Public buses connect major areas
