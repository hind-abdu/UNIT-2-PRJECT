Historical Sites

Masmak Fortress

This clay and mud-brick fort played a crucial role in the unification of Saudi Arabia. Today, it houses a museum that showcases artifacts and exhibits from the unification era.

Open daily 8AM - 9PM

Diriyah

The original home of the Saudi royal family and the first Saudi state. This UNESCO World Heritage site features restored mud-brick buildings, museums, and cultural centers.
